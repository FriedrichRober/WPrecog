  [1X2.1 [33X[0;0YWreath Product Decomposition[133X[101X
  
  [33X[0;0YThe  returned  embeddings,  i.e.  wreath  product  decompositions,  map into

  [10XWreathProduct[110X.  The  main  way for the User to look at a wreath product in a
